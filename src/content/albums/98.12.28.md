This record feels less like a captured performance and more like weather that happened to be preserved.

The performances stretch without losing tension. Repetition becomes atmosphere, and atmosphere becomes motion. That slow conversion is what makes the album so absorbing.

I come back to it when I want music that changes the shape of an evening rather than simply filling it.
