*Vespertine* still feels like an argument for scale. So much of it is miniature: tiny clicks, breath, harp, voice. Yet none of that makes the record feel small.

What keeps pulling me back is the patience of the arrangement. Every sound seems placed with enough care that the gaps between sounds begin to matter as much as the sounds themselves.

It is one of the records I return to when I want proof that intimacy can carry real force without needing volume.
